Scroll Journey (6 Rooms)

Room 0

Hero / Supernova

Camera inside the supernova. Title + subtitle on glass HUD. Scroll begins to pull camera outward, revealing the particle network.

Room 1

Overview / About

Full topology visible. About text on glass panel. Camera slowly orbits. Supernova pulses gently in center.

Room 2

Topology Deep Dive

Scroll zooms into each node cluster sequentially. Glass panels show node details (publicCopy, claims). Supernova intensifies behind active node.

Room 3

Projects Showcase

Camera pulls back to overview. Project cards appear as floating glass panels. Click triggers portal transition to HTML project page.

Room 4

Tech Stack

Camera orbits to a different angle. Tech icons/labels orbit the topology as satellites. Glass panel with stack details.

Room 5

Contact / End

Camera zooms back into supernova core. Contact info on glass HUD. Full circle — you end where you began.

Technology Stack

3D Engine

@react-three/fiber, @react-three/drei, @react-three/postprocessing

Scroll Control

@react-three/drei ScrollControls

Maps scroll position to camera path + room transitions

Particles

GPU-driven particle system for nodes, edges, supernova, and dust

Post-processing

Bloom + Vignette + Noise

UnrealBloomPass for glow, subtle film grain for texture
